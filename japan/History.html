Japan's History'

Japan's history in the past had alot about samurais as they were a dominant ruling class

Muromachi period which start in 1336 and ended in 1573 which was known for the rule of the Ashikaga shogunate, which happened in kyoto the period ended when the Oda Nobunaga a warlord expelled the last Ashikaga shogun, Ashikaga Yoshiaki, from Kyoto.

Azuchi-Momoyama period which started in 1568 and ended in 1600 it started when emperor Oda Nobunaga's entry into Kyoto in 1568 and ended in 1600 when the Battle of Sekigahara by Tokugawa the clash rivary

Kamakura 1185-1333 it started in 1185 when the Minamoto clan's had conquered and defeated the Taira clan in the Genpei War. It ended in 1333 during the Genkō War, a rebellion led by Emperor Go-Daigo who wanted to restore imperial power, which successfully overthrowed the ruling Hōjō clan and the Kamakura Shogunate through sieges, culminating in the Hōjō leaders' suicide after forces led by samurai like Nitta Yoshisada attacked the capital.
