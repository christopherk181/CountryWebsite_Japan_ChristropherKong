Japanese Food

Here are Japans's best Kinds of there food its also known as washoku which centers on rice, seasonal ingredients, and fresh flavors

Ichigo Daifuku is a fresh, whole strawberry, wrapped in a layer of sweet red bean paste (anko), and then covered in a soft, chewy mochi (glutinous rice flour) dough, often dusted with cornstarch or potato starch to prevent sticking.

Takoyaki which is made of a wheat flour-based batter filled with ingredients like octopus, green onion, and tempura scraps, which is then cooked in a special molded pan.

Sushi which is made of rice and other meat like crab meat shrimp or vegetables and wrapped with seaweed.
